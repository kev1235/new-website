Silver Jewelry

Welcome to the page about silver jewelry.

Silver is a highly desirable precious metal that is used to make numerous categories of jewelry. Many people prefer jewelry made with sterling silver. Sterling silver is a combination of pure silver with a small amount of different metals added to increase the strength.

Photo Credit: https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Silver_Jewelry_in_Dharamsala.jpg

Although silver is a rare and expensive metal, it is less expensive than gold. The price of silver jewelry is usually less than the price of gold jewelry. Silver is a beautiful metal to use in jewelry since it produces bright reflections when exposed to light.
