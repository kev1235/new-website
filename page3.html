Necklaces

Welcome to the page about necklaces.

Necklaces are popular jewelry items that are worn around the neck. Decorative necklaces are worn by people attending special formal events, such as weddings and banquets.

Photo Credit: https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Blue_Sapphire_%26_Diamond_Necklace.jpg

Necklaces are made in a variety of different sizes.
